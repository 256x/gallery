# Literal Gallery User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Launch the app
2. Grant photo/video access when prompted
3. Your grid appears immediately, grouped by month and day

There's no folder to pick and nothing to configure — every photo and video on the device shows up automatically.

## The Grid

Thumbnails are grouped by date:

- **Month header** — sticky at the top while you scroll through that month
- **Day label** — marks each day's group beneath the month header

Pinch in to show more columns (smaller thumbnails), pinch out to show fewer (larger thumbnails). The grid remembers your last size.

Tap any thumbnail to open it full-screen.

## Viewing a Photo or Video

- **Swipe left/right** — move to the next or previous item, across the whole library
- **Pinch / drag** — zoom and pan a photo
- **Swipe up** — reveal EXIF info (camera, exposure, ISO, f-number, resolution, file size). Videos show duration, resolution, date, and size instead
- **Share icon (top right)** — send the photo or video to another app

Videos play automatically when they become the active page and pause when you swipe away.

## What gets included

Everything MediaStore has indexed on the device: camera photos, screenshots, downloads, videos, and anything saved by other apps. There's no per-folder or per-app filter — it's a single flat timeline sorted by date.
